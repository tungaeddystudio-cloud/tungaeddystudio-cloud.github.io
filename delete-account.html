Delete Your Account

Permanently delete your SweatLock account and all associated data. This cannot be undone. The fastest way is directly inside the app.

✓ Fastest — Delete In-App (Instant)
1

Open SweatLock on your Android device

2

Go to Settings tab (bottom navigation bar)

3

Scroll to the bottom → tap "Delete Account"

4

Confirm — your account is deleted instantly

⚠ What gets permanently deleted
  • Firebase Auth account
  • All stats: reps, streaks, unlocks, Sweat Score
  • Profile: username and avatar
  • All earned badges and tier progress
  • Leaderboard entry
  • All local data on this device

Delete Your Account

Permanently delete your SweatLock account and all data. This is irreversible. We recommend using the in-app option first — it's instant and requires no waiting.

✓ Fastest — Delete In-App (Instant)
1

Open SweatLock on your Android device

2

Go to the Settings tab (bottom navigation bar)

3

Scroll to the bottom → tap "Delete Account"

4

Confirm the dialog — your account is deleted instantly

⚠ What gets permanently deleted
  • Firebase Auth account (anonymous or Google-linked)
  • All stats: total reps, streaks, unlocks, Sweat Score
  • Profile: username and avatar
  • All earned badges and tier progress
  • Leaderboard entry and ranking
  • All local app data on this device

Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your SweatLock account and all associated data. This action is irreversible.

✅ Faster: Delete In-App

1

Open SweatLock on your phone

2

Go to Settings tab (bottom navigation)

3

Scroll to the bottom → tap "Delete Account"

4

Confirm the deletion — account is deleted instantly

⚠️ What gets permanently deleted

  • Firebase Auth account (Anonymous or Google-linked)
  • All stats: reps, streaks, unlocks, Sweat Score
  • Profile: username, avatar, badges
  • Leaderboard entry
  • All local app data on this device

🔒 SweatLock Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 26, 2024 | Last Updated: March 2025

Summary: SweatLock processes camera data locally on your device for exercise detection. We do not store, record, or transmit your camera footage. Basic profile and exercise stats are synced to Firebase to preserve your progress across devices. You can delete your account and all data at any time.

1. Introduction

Welcome to SweatLock ("we," "our," or "us"). SweatLock is a fitness discipline application that requires users to complete physical exercises before accessing social media apps. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using SweatLock, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Camera Data (Required Permission)

Important: Camera access is used ONLY for real-time pose detection during exercises. All processing happens locally on your device using Google ML Kit. No images or videos are ever recorded or transmitted.

We display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect advertising ID and general region for ad personalization. You can opt out via device Google settings.

3. Authentication Methods

SweatLock supports two authentication methods:

  • Anonymous Auth: Automatically created on first launch. No email required. Data is tied to the device and UID.
  • Google Sign-In: Optional. Links your Google account for cross-device sync and data recovery. Email and display name are stored.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Core Functionality: Detect exercises and count repetitions
  • Progress Tracking: Show exercise history, streaks, and badges
  • Leaderboard: Display your Sweat Score ranking against other users
  • Badge System: Award tier upgrades and hidden badges based on activity
  • Cross-Device Sync: Restore progress if you reinstall the app
  • App Improvement: Fix bugs and improve performance
  • Advertising: Display relevant ads to support free app access

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal data.

We share data only with:

  • Firebase / Google: For authentication, database, analytics, and crash reporting
  • Google AdMob: For advertising purposes
  • Leaderboard: Your username, avatar, and Sweat Score are visible to other SweatLock users on the leaderboard
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law

6. Data Retention

  • Firebase data: Retained until account deletion is requested
  • Local data: Stored until app uninstall or "Clear Data" in device settings
  • Analytics data: Retained by Firebase per their policies (typically 14 months)
  • Camera data: Never stored — real-time only

7. Account & Data Deletion

You have the right to permanently delete your account and all associated data.

Option 1 — In-App Deletion (Recommended)

  1. Open SweatLock
  2. Go to Settings tab
  3. Scroll to bottom → tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion

This permanently deletes your Firebase Auth account, all Firestore data (profile, stats, badges, leaderboard entry), and clears local SharedPreferences.

Option 2 — Web Request

If you cannot access the app, submit a deletion request here:

Request Account Deletion

We will process your request within 30 days and confirm via email.

Option 3 — Clear Local Data Only

  1. Device Settings → Apps → SweatLock
  2. Tap "Clear Data" or "Clear Storage"

Note: This clears local data only. Firebase data persists until full account deletion.

8. Your Rights

  • Access: View your data within the app
  • Deletion: Delete account via in-app or web request
  • Correction: Edit username and avatar in Profile settings
  • Opt-out of ads: Via Google Settings on device
  • Revoke permissions: Via device settings (app functionality may be limited)

9. Data Security

  • Firebase Firestore security rules prevent users from reading or modifying other users' data
  • Users cannot decrease their own stats (anti-cheat protection in Firestore rules)
  • HTTPS for all network communication
  • Camera processing is 100% on-device
